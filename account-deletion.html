Account Deletion Request – KKSA

This page explains how users of the KKSA mobile application can request deletion of their account and all associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your KKSA account, please send an email to:

Email: sonadeveloper8@gmail.com

Your email request should include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your registered phone number or email (used inside the app)
  • Subject: Request to Delete KKSA Account

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once your deletion request is processed, the following user data will be permanently deleted:

  • Profile information
  • Login credentials
  • Uploaded photos
  • QR scan history (if stored)
  • Any other personalized data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or audit obligations (for example, transaction logs required by law).

How Long Will It Take?

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7–14 business days.
